About Me

Hello, I'm Will.

I'm a final-year student studying Mobile Application Design & Development at Algonquin College. Even today, it amazes me that we carry around exceptionally powerful computers in our pockets. As a result, I am obsessed with building applications for mobile devices. When I'm not hunkered down in my code dungeon, I enjoy long-distance running with my wife, playing the trombone, and relaxing with our two cats, Pixel and Sassy.
