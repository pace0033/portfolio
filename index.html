About Me

Hello, I'm Will!

I'm a Mobile Software Engineer in my final semester studying Mobile Application Design & Development at Algonquin College.

When I bought the first-generation iPhone, I felt like I was holding a reality-defying device that was equal parts magic and science fiction. That sense of wonder and awe has followed me into the present day, where I remain fascinated by the exceptionally powerful computers we carry around in our pockets.

When I'm not hunkered down in my code dungeon, I enjoy long-distance running with my wife, playing the trombone, and relaxing with our two cats, Pixel and Sassy.
